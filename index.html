Fight Club

Cast: Brad Pitt, Edward Norton. Helena Bonham Carter. Director: David Fincher

A depressed man meets a strange soap salesman in a plane trip named Tyler Durden and we then see him living in Tyler's wrecked and old house after his apartment got destroyed by a explosive. The two men who were bored decided to form an underground fighting group for the men who are tired of their boring and repetitive lives and want to feel fulfilled again.

Rating: IMDb: 8.8/10, "Fight Club did indeed fly over the heads of audiences expecting straight macho action thrills, as opposed to a mind-messing critique of the same, as well as capitalism, material acquisition, masculinity and other sacred cows of national identity."- Dennis Harvey Rotten Tomatoes

Taxi Driver

Cast: Robert De Niro, Jodie Foster, Cybill Shepherd, Harvey Keitel. Director: Martin Scorsese

A lone veteran named Travis Bickle decides to be a cab driver and the more he's out at night the more he wishes to clean up the filthy city he lives in. After meeting a campaign worker he gets the idea of saving the city but first plots to assassinate a presidential candidate but decided to save a 12 year old prostitute from evil gangsters

Ratings: IMDb: 8.2/10, ""Taxi Driver" shouldn't be taken as a New York film; it's not about a city but about the weathers of a man's soul, and out of all New York he selects just those elements that feed and reinforce his obsessions. The man is Travis Bickle, ex-Marine, veteran of Vietnam, composer of dutiful anniversary notes to his parents, taxi driver, killer. The movie rarely strays very far from the personal, highly subjective way in which he sees the city and lets it wound him."- Roger Ebert

No Country For Old Men

Cast: Javier Bordem, Josh Brolin, Tommy Lee Jones, Woody Harrelson. Director: The Coen Brothers

While Llewelyn Moss was out hunting, he finds the brutal aftermath of a drug deal gone wrong and even though he knows the better decision, he still steals the suitcase full of money but little did he know that his fate was sealed because there was a hitman named Anton Chigurh looking for the money. There is also a Sheriff who wants to help and protect Moss from the people trying to kill him

Ratings: IMDb: 8.2/10, "An Instant Classic"
